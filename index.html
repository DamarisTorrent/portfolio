Career Goal

My vision for the future of government technology

My career goal is to become a recognized expert in AI-driven government modernization, serving as a bridge between cutting-edge artificial intelligence and the critical systems that serve millions of Americans. I envision myself leading transformative initiatives that take aging government codebases, some decades old, and revitalize them using AI-powered analysis and automated refactoring in a fraction of the traditional timeline.

Just as the ocean has taught me patience and respect for powerful forces, I approach legacy systems with both reverence for their critical function and excitement for their potential evolution. I want to be the person agencies call when they need to modernize responsibly, someone who understands both the technical complexity and the human impact of these systems.

Ultimately, I aim to help government work better for everyone by making AI accessible, ethical, and genuinely useful for public servants and the citizens they serve.
