Where Technology Meets the Tide

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I'm Charlotte Damaris Torrent, a software developer based in Charleston, South Carolina, where the rhythm of the ocean influences everything I do. Just as the tide constantly reshapes the shoreline, I believe technology should continuously evolve to better serve people.

My journey in tech has been anything but linear. From pioneering remote work in the late 1990s at a 600-person organization to now building AI platforms that serve U.S. government agencies, I've learned that the best solutions come from persistence, adaptability, and a willingness to dive into uncharted waters.

When I'm not architecting cloud solutions or training AI models, you'll find me catching waves at Folly Beach or mesmerized by the intricate ecosystems in my home aquariums. There's something profound about watching complex systems work in harmony, whether it's a coral reef or a well-designed software architecture.
